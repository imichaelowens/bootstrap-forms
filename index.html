Do you know about German cars?

Millions of people around the world drive German cars. Arguably the most famous, the Volkswagen Beetle designed by Ferdinand Porsche, became one of best setting cars of all time only second to the Toyota Corolla. Germany's contribution to cars does not end there. The inventor of the modern car has since been disputed between three Germans, Karl Benz and Gottlieb Daimler of Mercedes-Benz and a Jewish inventor in the late 1800's who used a gasoline engine to power a cart know as Siegfried Marcus.
